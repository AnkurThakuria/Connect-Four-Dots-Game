This is the Connect Four Game

The rules are: Each player may drop only one checker into the grid per turn. A winner is declared when one player gets four of their colored checkers in a row vertically, horizontally or diagonally. Other configurations, such as squares, don't count as winning combinations.

Let's Play!

The object of this game is to connect four of your chips in a row!
